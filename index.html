QUESTIONS YOU'D ASK ANYWAY

Before you write to us

Will you break our current tracking while fixing it?

No. Everything is built in parallel to your live setup and verified in GA4 DebugView, Tag Assistant and Events Manager before anything is switched over. Your reporting never goes dark — the old setup keeps running until the new one is proven.

How fast do we see results?

Deduplication and consent fixes show up in platform dashboards within days — you'll see conversion counts move. Algorithm improvements (smarter bidding on cleaner signals) compound over 2–4 weeks as Google and Meta re-learn on the corrected data.

Do we need to involve our developers?

Usually 0–2 hours of their time in total. Most of the work happens in GTM, checkout settings and webhooks. The typical developer asks: add one DNS record for the tracking subdomain, and review a dataLayer snippet. We write exact, copy-pasteable instructions for both.

What access do you need?

Analyst or editor-level access to GA4, GTM and your ad platforms — we send an exact checklist with the minimum roles required. Never billing or admin access. Everything we change is documented, so any competent specialist can audit or reverse it.

Is server-side tracking legal under GDPR?

Yes — when consent is respected. Server-side tagging changes where data is processed (your own subdomain, your own EU server), not whether you're allowed to collect it. We wire Consent Mode v2 signals through the whole chain, so opted-out users stay opted out — provably.

What if the audit finds nothing wrong?

Then you get a written report proving your setup is solid — with the evidence to show your team, agency or investors. That has literally never happened in 115+ projects, but the offer stands.
